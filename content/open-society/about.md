I'm a graphic and product designer who has spent **10+ years working with the teams
defending human rights, equity, and open society:** nonprofits, research institutions,
and women-led initiatives, including organizations like digitalundivided and the
Rainforest Foundation US.

My work sits at the intersection of design and purpose, and I've focused it on two
things a foundation like Open Society needs most: **advocacy communications that
move people to act, and impact reporting that makes complex research legible to
funders, policymakers, and the public.** From annual reports to data visualization
to campaign assets, I build the visual system that carries your findings from the
research team to the people who need to hear them.

I work closely with my clients, not as a contractor, but as a partner. **When we
build together, we create work neither of us could have imagined alone.**
