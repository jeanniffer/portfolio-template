I'm a graphic and product designer who has spent **10+ years working with the teams that are quietly changing things:** nonprofits, EdTech companies, artists, and women-led initiatives.

My work has always lived at the intersection of design and purpose. I fell in love with educational spaces and social impact organizations early in my career... the community, the mission, the way design can amplify a message that needs to be heard.

I work closely with my clients, not as a contractor, but as a partner. **When we build together, we create work neither of us could have imagined alone.**
